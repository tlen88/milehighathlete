How publishing works

  1. Edit — open a section above and change the text or images.
  2. Save — your change is stored as a draft. The live site is untouched.
  3. Publish — set the draft's status to “Ready”, then choose Publish. The website rebuilds itself automatically.
  4. Check — after a couple of minutes, refresh milehighathlete.com and confirm your change is live.
